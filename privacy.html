When you register for an account or use our Services, we may collect:

When you use the Services, we automatically collect certain information:

We may receive information about you from third parties, including social media platforms, app store providers, and analytics services.

We may collect certain categories of sensitive information:

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Breach Notification

  • Assessment: Determine scope, nature, and impact

  • Regulatory Reporting: Notify relevant authorities as required by law

Breach notifications will include: description of what happened, types of information compromised, steps we’re taking, steps you can take, and contact information.
