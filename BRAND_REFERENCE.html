✗ NEVER

  • Place on busy photographic backgrounds
  • Stretch, skew, or distort the logo
  • Change colours of logo or symbol
  • Add drop shadows, outlines, or effects
  • Use below minimum size
  • Recreate or retype the logotype

At the heart of everything we say

Friendly

Approachable, relatable, likeable

NEVER WRITE: optimize · seamless · leverage · ecosystem · AI-powered (as primary claim) · pain points (customer-facing) · "the app" · any shame language · "Sarah from Denver" · invented testimonials · invented statistics
ALWAYS SAY: easyChef Pro (full name every time) · "Your kitchen, in command." · "Less waste. Less stress. More dinner." · "The invisible leak" · "Dinner stops being a daily crisis." · "Built by first responders" · "The loop closes." · "One app. One loop."
The Three Tests — run every line through these:
  1. Would she text this to a friend? If not — rewrite it.
  2. Does it sound like a pitch deck? If yes — start over.
  3. Does it feel like a lecture? If yes — cut it.

11 — Photography Style (for Image Briefs)

✓ DO USE

  • Overhead / flat-lay food photography
  • Natural, warm lighting with soft shadows
  • Fresh, real ingredients — not stock imagery
  • Intimate pantry and kitchen scenes
  • Authentic, unposed lifestyle moments
  • Warm cream / neutral backgrounds
  • Real family dinner table moments

✗ NEVER USE

  • Heavily processed or over-filtered images
  • Harsh direct flash or cold clinical lighting
  • Generic stock photos of phones or apps
  • Cluttered, chaotic kitchen scenes
  • Dark or low-contrast photography
  • Corporate, posed, or formal imagery
  • Blue tones anywhere
