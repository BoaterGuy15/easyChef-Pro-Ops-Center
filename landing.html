Objection Handling

Still have questions?

We’ve heard every hesitation. Here’s the honest answer to each one.

QIsn’t this just another meal planning app?
No. Meal planners plan. easyChef Pro plans, tracks, optimizes, and shops — all in one closed loop. It’s not where you find recipes. It’s how you run your kitchen.
QHow do you know I’ll save $1,336?
That’s our projection. We determined this through a simulated study comprising 10,000 households. Your exact savings depend on your household size and current waste habits.
QWhat if my family doesn’t use it?
They don’t have to. easyChef Pro is designed for one person to lead. You manage the app, your family benefits. They eat the meals. You run the kitchen.
QDo I have to log my entire pantry?
No. Tell us three things in your fridge and we’ll decide tonight’s dinner. Your full pantry naturally populates as you shop and cook.
QIs my data safe?
Yes. Deterministic FDA-grade data, not AI that trains on your personal information. Your kitchen stays yours.
QWhen do I actually get the app?
July 1, 2026. Three years of development. Nine patent-pending technologies. Built by people who don’t ship until it’s right.
